Intro

I would liken working with data to a kid playing in a sandbox, with endless possibilities of what can be created. My interest arose from my petroleum engineering background as it is a data-abundant industry. My curiosity led me to ask more questions and I have been on an exponential learning curve since.

I have spent the past few years immersing myself in the data-world by learning and doing projects to build data-driven solutions. In the process, I have gained knowledge and skills in SQL, data analysis, machine learning, natural language processing and python with libraries such as Sklearn, OpenCV, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib and Seaborn. By the way, check out my awesome work.

Projects

1. Glass Ballot

This was a group project to build a solution using AI and blockchain technology to streamline the election monitoring process. The electoral process is conducted on the NEAR Protocol blockchain network to build transparency of the voting process. AI was used to enhance the security of the system by detecting attacks before they happen.

The solution was second place in the IndabaX Kenya 2022 hackathon.

2. OCR

The Communications Authority of Kenya gave a directive to telecommunication companies in Kenya to re-register their customers. The team at Gearbox Academy built a website to enable the customers to register their phone numbers online without the hassle of doing it at a physical shop.

The OCR project is a feature in the sim card registration project. The OCR will be used to extract the customerâ€™s personal identification details from their identification documents and stored on a customer database of the respective telecommunication companies.
